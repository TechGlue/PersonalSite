Coming up as a first generation American I didn't have much exposure to code growing up. I found my love for code by solving common tech problems that I had. Going into my senior year of high school my Science teacher introduced me to Computer Science as a career path after highschool. At first I was skeptical about it since I never heard about it and I had the stigma that it was for those who exceled at school. I decided to try it out because it had two of my favorite things in it tech and solving problems. Ever since then, I've been hooked learning new technologies and tools!
